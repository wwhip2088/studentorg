Active Brother Spotlight


Meet John Kubinak

Brother John Kubinak is a junior from New Jersey, and is one of the most active members not only of our local Alpha Epsilon Pi chapter, but of the entire Washington University community. As a member of Alpha Epsilon Pi, John has served in a number of official roles, including serving on just about every committee we have such as Social Committee and Pledge Committee, as well as leading them. John is also one of our most active brothers in Thurtene construction. In addition to being essential to the every day operation of our chapter, John is involved in most successful business in the Washington University Student Entrepreneurial Program (StEP), University Trucking, Inc. Utrucking does shipping and storage for over 1,400 WashU students a year from all over the United States. John is also involved with LINUS each semester, serving on their board. LINUS is a open-bar charity event each semester that raises thousands of dollars a year. John does all of this while studying Systems Engineering with a second major in Finance. Not suprisingly, John will be interning with Houlihan Lokey, an investment banking firm, out of Minneapolis this summer.
