Who We Are

The Alpha Epsilon Pi chapter at Washington University was chartered in 1928 as the 17th chapter of Alpha Epsilon Pi, thus making us the Sigma chapter following the Greek alphabet. Our chapter currently consists of 94 active members, after recently inducting our newest pledge class of 21 members. As a chapter, we are committed to leaving a positive impact on our local community here at Washington University, as well as supporting the greater international Alpha Epsilon Pi community. Pictured above is our current fraternity, university-owned housing on the North Side of the Washington University Campus, just across from The Village dining hall. We encourage you to explore the rest of this website to learn a little more about us! Thanks!
